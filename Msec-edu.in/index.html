About MSEC

Excellence in engineering education since 2001

Our Legacy of Excellence

Meenakshi Sundararajan Engineering College (MSEC) was established by the IIET Society in 2001. This institution is a part of the prestigious KRS Group of Institutions which also includes the renowned IIET (Indian Institute of Engineering Technology) established in 1947 by our Founder Late Shri K.R.Sundararajan, the well-known Meenakshi College for Women and the more recently established Meenakshi Sundararajan School of Management.

The institutions on the KRS Campus are known for the quality education they impart and their stringent levels of discipline. We have consistently outshone all our peers, not only in academics, but in co-curricular activities as well.

Vision & Mission

Guiding principles that drive our institution

Our Vision

To develop this institution into a "CENTRE OF EXCELLENCE" imparting Engineering education to young men and women, grooming their overall personality with the highest emphasis on ethical values.

We aim to create engineers who are not only technically proficient but also ethically responsible and ready to face the challenges of the industry and the nation at large.

Our Mission

  • Provide quality education with state-of-the-art infrastructure
  • Foster innovation, research, and entrepreneurship
  • Develop industry-ready professionals with strong ethical values
  • Promote holistic development through co-curricular activities
  • Maintain stringent levels of discipline and academic excellence
