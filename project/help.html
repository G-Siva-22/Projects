Help & Support Guide

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register for an event?

To register for an event, navigate to the main page and scroll down to the "Event Registration" section. Fill out the registration form with your details and select the event you wish to attend.

Can I cancel my registration?

Yes, you can cancel your registration by contacting us via email or phone at least 48 hours before the event starts.

How can I find events near me?

Click the "Find Nearby Events" button on the homepage. This will use your device's location to show events in your area.

Are there any fees for using this portal?

No, the portal is completely free to use. However, some events may have admission fees, which will be clearly indicated in the event details.

Privacy & Data

We value your privacy and handle all personal data in accordance with our Privacy Policy. Your information is only used to process event registrations and to provide you with relevant updates about community events.

You can clear your saved preferences at any time by clicking the "Clear Preferences" button on the registration form.
